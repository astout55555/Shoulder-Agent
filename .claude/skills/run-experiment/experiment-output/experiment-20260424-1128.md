## Interpretation (N=2 demo only)

N=2 per agent is too small for any single-run statistical claim; effects at this N are dominated by run-to-run variance. The point of this run is demonstration, not inference. That said, the directional pattern matches the established post-optimization signal:

- Reasoning-depth delta is positive on 3 of 4 scenarios; Accept promotion flips slightly negative (−0.003) within noise.
- Advice-bias delta is positive on all 4 scenarios, with the largest margin on Move to new city (+0.131) and Accept promotion (+0.163) — consistent with the pattern that emotionally ambiguous and lopsided scenarios benefit most from the adversarial structure.
- No zero-score failures; all 16 scored outputs completed cleanly.

The aggregate (+0.033 RD, +0.098 Bias) is in line with the pooled post-optimization signal from prior experiments (+0.025 and +0.076 respectively).
